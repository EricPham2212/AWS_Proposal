On this page, you will need to introduce your worklog. How did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? What did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

Week 1: Account Setup and Basic Networking (Modules 1 & 2)

Week 2: Advanced Storage and Compute Introduction (Modules 3 & 4)

Week 3: Advanced Compute and Databases (Module 4 & Databases)

Week 4: Expanded Databases and Monitoring (Databases & Monitoring)

Week 5: Server and Application Migration (Module 2)

Week 6: Database Migration and Hybrid Networking (Module 2)

Week 7: Cost Optimization and Performance (Module 3)

Week 8: Reliability and Advanced Security

Week 9: Microservices and Serverless

Week 10: Container Orchestration

Week 11: Data Lake and Analytics

Week 12: AI/ML Foundations
